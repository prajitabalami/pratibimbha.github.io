About Us

Pratibimba Incorporation aka pb Inc., is a creative think tank that stands as a catalyst for innovation and provides a platform for diverse individuals to collaborate, represent and challenge everyday thinking, and explore unique ideas to tackle the problems and opportunities of our rapidly changing world. Through interdisciplinary collaborations, we aim to create a space where people can come together, exchange ideas, and forge meaningful connections that transcend boundaries. Academic research, blogs, and visual arts are our key mediums to shed light on diverse narratives, untold histories, and alternative perspectives, allowing a nuanced understanding of our society. We are an ecosystem that celebrates diversity, connects people, and harnesses the power of storytelling and artistic expression to create a more reflective, empathetic, and socially conscious world.

Vision | Mission | Goals

Pratibimba Inc. envisions bringing forward innovative ideas and evidence-based research to drive positive social and economic change. We hope to create an environment where diverse voices are amplified and connect society through the power of artistic expression and interdisciplinary collaborations. We strive to be a leading creative think tank that uses various mediums, including academic research, opinionated writings, visual arts, and more, to bring forth unique stories and reflect the diverse tapestry of our society.

Our mission at Pratibimba Inc. is to generate and disseminate cutting-edge knowledge, insights, and policy recommendations that empower decision-makers, stakeholders, and the public to address complex societal issues. We aim to harness the transformative power of creativity and interdisciplinary collaboration to connect people, provoke meaningful conversations, and inspire positive change by creating a platform where artists, policymakers, academicians, and innovators can come together to explore, express, and share their perspectives on diverse nuances of our society.

Amplify Diverse Voices

We are committed to amplifying the voices of underrepresented communities, marginalised groups, and diverse perspectives through our platform. By actively seeking out and promoting the work of academicians, researchers, artists, entrepreneurs, government, civil society, experts and professionals from different backgrounds, we aim to create a more inclusive narrative that reflects the rich tapestry of society.

Conduct Research

Pratibimba Inc. aims to conduct high-quality, evidence-based research on a wide range of topics such as public policy, economics, social sciences, arts and media, technology, and environmental sustainability. Through rigorous research methodologies and interdisciplinary approaches, we seek to generate insights that inform policies and actions.

Disseminate Knowledge

We are committed to sharing our research findings, insights, and analyses with a wide audience. Through publications, reports, conferences, exhibitions, and digital platforms, we aim to contribute to public discourse and create awareness about pressing issues, promoting informed decision-making and public engagement.

Cultivate Creativity

Pratibimba Inc. aims to nurture creativity in all its forms, encouraging thinkers to push boundaries, challenge norms, and explore innovative approaches to storytelling and expression. By providing a supportive environment and resources, we aim to foster a vibrant creative community that sparks new ideas and perspectives.

Tell Unique Stories

We aspire to bring forth unique stories that often go unheard or unnoticed. Through visual arts, writing, academic research, and other mediums, we aim to shed light on diverse narratives, untold histories, and alternative perspectives, allowing for a more nuanced and comprehensive understanding of our society.

Reflect and Inspire

Pratibimba Inc. aims to reflect the social fabric and complexities of our world while inspiring positive change. By using creativity as a tool for reflection, critique, and inspiration, we strive to spark conversations, challenge societal norms, and contribute to a more just, inclusive, and compassionate society.

Foster Collaboration

We strive to foster collaboration among diverse stakeholders including policymakers, academicians, industry experts, artists, civil society organisations, and the public. Through interdisciplinary collaborations, workshops, events, and online platforms, we aim to facilitate dialogues, knowledge-sharing, and partnerships, to create a collective and inclusive approach to problem-solving and decision-making.

Influence Policy

Pratibimba Inc. seeks to play an influential role in shaping policy agendas at local, national, and international levels. We aim to provide evidence-based recommendations and policy briefs to policymakers, advocating for sustainable and equitable policies that address societal challenges.

Drive Innovation

Pratibimba Inc. aspires to be at the forefront of innovative ideas and approaches. We encourage exploration, experimentation, and the application of emerging technologies and methodologies to advance our research studies, and contribute to innovative solutions that address complex societal problems.
